"Welcome to a new way of exploring the world — where every step, every journey, and every adventure creates your own digital legacy. This app transforms real-world movement into an exciting experience by combining maps, fitness tracking, and a territory-based gaming system.
Track your walking, running, cycling, and travel activities while discovering new places. As you explore and complete routes, you can create your own Empire by capturing areas on the map. Give your Empire a unique identity, add your name, and show the world the places you have conquered.
But the adventure doesn't stop there. Other explorers can visit and challenge your territory. When they travel through your captured areas, your Empire can become weaker and new explorers can claim it. The more you move, explore, and stay active, the stronger your Empire becomes.
Compete with friends, climb leaderboards, unlock achievements, and become a legendary explorer. Whether you are walking around your city, running in a park, or discovering new places, every movement matters.
This is not just a map app — it is a real-world adventure game where your actions create your story.
Features:
• Real-time location tracking
• Walking, running, and travel monitoring
• Create and capture your own Empires
• Explore new areas and compete with others
• Leaderboards and achievements
• Personal profile and progress tracking
• Turn everyday movement into a fun challenge
Explore. Move. Capture. Build your Empire." 👑🌍
